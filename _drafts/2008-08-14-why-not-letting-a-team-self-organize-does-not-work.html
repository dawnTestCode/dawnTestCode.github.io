--- layout: post title: Why not letting a team self-organize does not work date: '2008-08-14T20:20:00.001-04:00' author: Dawn tags: modified_time: '2008-08-14T20:30:37.675-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6005334108330583722.post-5694175796574036316 --- What does it mean to let a team self-organize? I am guessing that it varies some from team to team, but I tend to think of concepts like managers trusting the team's opinion on what they need to get things done, empowering them and giving them the freedom to determine their best composition, structure, and timelines.
