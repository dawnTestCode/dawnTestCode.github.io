It's just how I am. There is background that makes this natural for me. And that's okay. Somehow, though, I still seem to be able to manage being a girly girl, too. I am not a brutish woman, I wear makeup, jewelry, and dresses whenever I can. I am almost *always* in a pair of heels.

At this point, this is easy for me, but I've come to realize that it may not seem quite so natural to other people, especially women. So I thought I'd just chat about how it goes for me.
